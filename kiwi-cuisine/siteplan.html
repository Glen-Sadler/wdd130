Overview

Purpose

This website aims to introduce and invite visitors to experience the rich and diverse world of iconic New Zealand cuisine. From traditional Māori delicacies to locally-sourced, fresh ingredients unique to Aotearoa, our goal is to share the flavours and cultural significance of New Zealand’s beloved foods. Whether you’re a local or a visitor, we hope to inspire you to explore and savour the culinary heritage that makes New Zealand a truly remarkable destination.

Audience

Locals: New Zealand residents seeking a deeper connection to their culinary heritage or discovering new local delicacies. International Visitors and Food Tourists: Travelers and food enthusiasts eager to explore iconic New Zealand foods as part of their cultural experience, looking for places to taste traditional dishes and learn more about local ingredients. Food Enthusiasts and Culinary Adventurers: Those with a passion for culinary arts who want to expand their palate and knowledge of international flavors, discovering how New Zealand’s unique geography and cultural influences shape its cuisine. Home Cooks and Foodies: Those who enjoy recreating international flavors at home, looking for inspiration and recipes to bring a taste of New Zealand into their own kitchens.

Home page

Enjoy a rich and unique experience Explore and Savour the Aotearoa culinery excellence

We aim to introduce and invite visitors to experience the rich and diverse world of iconic New Zealand cuisine. From traditional Māori delicacies to locally-sourced, fresh ingredients unique to Aotearoa, our goal is to share the flavors and cultural significance of New Zealand’s beloved foods. Whether you’re a local or a visitor, we hope to inspire you to explore and savour the culinary heritage that makes New Zealand a truly remarkable destination.

The indigenous cuisine of Aotearoa, shaped by Māori culture, features a rich variety of ingredients and traditional cooking methods that honor the land and sea. The hāngi, an earth-cooked meal, includes meats and root vegetables like kūmara, while kaimoana (seafood) such as pāua, kina, and tītī bring unique coastal flavors to the table. Native plants like puha, used in hearty boil-ups, and edible seaweed like karengo add distinct local tastes, alongside rewena, a traditional Māori bread made with a fermented potato starter. These dishes reflect values of manaakitanga (hospitality) and kaitiakitanga (guardianship), emphasizing respect for natural resources. By preserving these foods, Māori culture sustains its identity and shares its rich culinary heritage.

Menu

Experience Authentic Indigenous Flavours Today Savour the Authentic Flavors of Our Menu

At our restaurant, we celebrate the rich culinary heritage of indigenous New Zealand with an array of exquisite dishes. Dive into the unique flavors of kina, with its creamy texture, or savor the tender salmon that reflects the pristine waters of our coastlines. Our green-lipped mussels are a must-try, showcasing the ocean’s bounty, while the hearty boil up, and hāngi offers a traditional taste of home. Indulge in succulent paua, known for its vibrant colors and rich flavor, and experience the rich, gamey taste of tītī. The embrace the unique experience of partaking of the iconic Kiwi pie.

Our menu also features tender lamb, flavorful beef, and juicy pork, alongside the renowned Bluff oysters, celebrated for their exceptional taste and freshness. Complement your meal with traditional sides such as fluffy rewana bread, enjoy fried bread, and a refreshing watercress salad. For a sweet finish, enjoy a steamed pudding with custard, lamingtons, assorted ice cream, creamy yogurt, and the classic pavlova. Each dish is a tribute to our land and sea, inviting you to savor the essence of Aotearoa.
