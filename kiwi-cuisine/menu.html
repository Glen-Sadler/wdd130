Experience Authentic Indigenous Flavours Today

Savour the Authentic Flavours of Our Menu

At our restaurant, we celebrate the rich culinary heritage of indigenous New Zealand with an array of exquisite dishes. Dive into the unique flavors of kina, with its creamy texture, or savour the tender salmon that reflects the pristine waters of our coastlines. Our green-lipped mussels are a must-try, showcasing the ocean’s bounty, while the hearty boil up, and hāngi offers a traditional taste of home. Indulge in succulent paua, known for its vibrant colors and rich flavor, and experience the rich, gamey taste of tītī. The embrace the unique experience of partaking of the iconic Kiwi pie.

Our menu also features tender lamb, flavorful beef, and juicy pork, alongside the renowned Bluff oysters, celebrated for their exceptional taste and freshness. Complement your meal with traditional sides such as fluffy rewana bread, enjoy fried bread, and a refreshing watercress salad. For a sweet finish, enjoy a steamed pudding with custard, lamingtons, assorted ice cream, creamy yogurt, and the classic pavlova. Each dish is a tribute to our land and sea, inviting you to savour the essence of Aotearoa.
